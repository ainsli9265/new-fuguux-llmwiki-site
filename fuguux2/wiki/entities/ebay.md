# eBay

An e-commerce/auction site cited as a positive example of [[customer-centered-design]]: built "from the ground up" around customer needs; uses a shaded, three-dimensional [[action-buttons|action button]] ("Register Now") as an effective call to action; and one of the major sites whose core interaction patterns stayed structurally stable from 2001 to 2006 even as visual styling evolved (see [[web-design-patterns]]).

In [[recommendation-community]], the worked example for a meta-rating system that scores trustworthiness of *people* rather than quality of a product: a simple +1 (positive), 0 (neutral), −1 (negative) score on buyers and sellers, distinct from Amazon's "was this review helpful?" approach to rating reviews themselves.
