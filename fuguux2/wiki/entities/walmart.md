# Walmart.com

An e-commerce site named only incidentally in an earlier ingestion (Pattern Group B's navigation-framework chapter), now the worked example for two checkout-extension patterns: [[multiple-destinations]] (offering a "Ship to Multiple Addresses" action button during checkout, then letting customers pick a destination per item from their address book) and [[gift-giving]] (a gift-options step that lets customers add gift wrap, a personalized message, and a gift receipt, with prices hidden from the recipient on the packing slip).
