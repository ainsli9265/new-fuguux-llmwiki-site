# IBM

Cited twice, each time with real substance rather than a passing name-drop. First, as the business-case example anchoring [[customer-centered-design]]: a customer-centered redesign of ibm.com drove a reported 400% jump in sales. Second, as the worked example in [[organized-search-results]] for handling search-term synonyms — IBM's site routes the queries "notebook," "laptop," and "ThinkPad" to the same destination, and additionally shows a special tailored promotional result for the common terms "notebook"/"laptop" alongside the standard search results, rather than replacing one with the other.
